# AutoStream Product Knowledge Base

## Company Overview
AutoStream is an automated video editing platform designed for content creators. We provide AI-powered tools to automatically edit, caption, and enhance videos for various platforms.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan - $29/month
- **Videos per month:** Up to 10
- **Resolution:** 720p maximum
- **AI Captions:** Not included
- **Support:** Standard email support (24-48 hour response)
- **Best for:** Individual creators just starting out

### Pro Plan - $79/month
- **Videos per month:** Unlimited
- **Resolution:** 4K (up to 4096x2160)
- **AI Captions:** Included (auto-generated subtitles in 50+ languages)
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support (live chat + phone)
- **Best for:** Professional creators and small studios

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- **No refunds issued after 7 days** from the date of purchase
- If you request a refund within 7 days of purchase, a full refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days
- This applies to both monthly and annual subscriptions

### Support Availability
- **Basic Plan:** Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM UTC (standard email support only)
- **Pro Plan:** 24 hours a day, 7 days a week (live chat, email, phone)
- **Response times:** 
  - Pro Plan: Average 5 minutes via chat, 30 minutes via email
  - Basic Plan: Average 24-48 hours via email

### Cancellation Policy
- Cancel anytime, no long-term contracts
- Your access continues until the end of the paid billing period
- No charges will be applied after cancellation

### Free Trial
- 7-day free trial of the Pro Plan available (no credit card required)
- After 7 days, plan defaults to Basic Plan ($0/month) unless upgraded
- Full access to all Pro features during trial

## Common Questions Answered

### How is billing handled?
Billing is monthly. You'll be charged on the same day each month. You can cancel anytime.

### Can I switch plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What video formats are supported?
We support: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM, FLV, WMV, MKV, and more.

### What's the max file size?
Maximum 5GB per video for direct upload. Larger files can be uploaded via Google Drive or Dropbox.

### Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes! Annual subscriptions are 15% off compared to monthly billing.

### How do I cancel?
Visit your account settings and click "Cancel Plan". You'll have access until the end of your billing period.
